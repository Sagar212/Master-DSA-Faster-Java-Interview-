# 🧠 Mental Story Visualization — Binary Search

> **ONE SENTENCE TO REMEMBER**: "Open a dictionary to the middle — too far? Go left half. Too early? Go right half. Repeat."

---

## 📗 Easy: Binary Search — "Dictionary lookup. Open middle → halve."
## 📙 Medium: Rotated Array — "Dropped dictionary. One half always sorted."
## 📕 Hard: Median of Two Arrays — "Two assembly lines. Binary search shorter. Partition so left ≤ right."

**Key formula**: `mid = left + (right - left) / 2` (overflow-safe)
