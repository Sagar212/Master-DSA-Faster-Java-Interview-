# 🧠 Mental Story Visualization — Stack / Queue

> **ONE SENTENCE TO REMEMBER**: "Stack = undo button (LIFO). Queue = waiting line (FIFO)."

---

## 📗 Easy: Valid Parentheses — "Matching socks. Opener → push. Closer → pop and compare."
## 📙 Medium: Daily Temperatures — "Waiting for warmer day. Monotonic stack of indices."
## 📕 Hard: Largest Rectangle — "Building skyline. Increasing stack. Shorter bar → pop taller, calc area."

**Key**: Monotonic stack stores INDICES not values. Pop when condition breaks.
